Parameter: xNoseGear¶

The x-position of the nose gear

Unit:[m]
Author:Patrick Goden, Technische Universitaet Hamburg Harburg, Master Thesis

Calculation Methods¶

xNoseGear.calc()¶

It is the average value of different aircraft types. The value is calculated by multiplying the statical quotient of the x-coordinate of the landing gear and the fuselage width by the fuselage width of the considered aircraft type.

Source:Different airfcraft types of Airbus and Boeing

Parameter: zLandingGear¶

The z-position of the landing gear

Unit:[m]
Author:Patrick Goden, Technische Universitaet Hamburg Harburg, Master Thesis

Calculation Methods¶

zLandingGear.calc()¶

Calculates the z-position of the landing gear with respekt to the required lift off angle (LoA) for a rotation without tail strike. This angle is estimated by a value of 12.5 degrees.

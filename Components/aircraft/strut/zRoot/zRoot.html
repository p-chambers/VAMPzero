Parameter: zRoot¶

The z position of the root leading edge of the strut.

Unit:[m]

Calculation Methods¶

zRoot.calc()¶

Calculates the z location of the strut root leading edge according to the fuselage geometry.

This calculation assumes that the strut is placed above the landing gear. The landing gear will extend at least as wide as the overall fuselage. Hence the strut is located at the lowermost position of the fuselage.

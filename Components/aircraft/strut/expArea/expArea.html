Parameter: expArea¶

The exposed area of the wing

Unit:[m2]
Wiki:http://adg.stanford.edu/aa241/wingdesign/winggeometry.html

Calculation Methods¶

expArea.calc()¶

Calculates the exposed Area by subtracting a trapezoid from the reference area The trapezoid has the lengths a = cRoot, c = cInter, where cInter is the chord at the fuselage/wing intersection and h = yfus, where yfus is the y coordinate of the wing/fuselage intersection

Parameter: zTip¶

The z position of the tip leading edge of the strut.

Unit:[m]

Calculation Methods¶

zTip.calc()¶

Calculates the z location of the strut tip leading edge according to the wing geometry.

The calculation places the strut tip near the front spar of the wing. Hence, it should benefit aerostatic and maybe aeroelastic behavior as it helps the wing to wash out.

Calculation is only valid if the strut is twisted around the dihedral axis

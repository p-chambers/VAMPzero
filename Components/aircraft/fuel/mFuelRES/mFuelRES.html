Parameter: mFuelRES¶

The fuel used in the reserve segment

Unit:[kg]

Calculation Methods¶

mFuelRES.calc()¶

Calculates the fuel mass used in the reserve segment. This is a simplified approach to the reserve fuel mass needed. It takes into account a climb from FL1500 to cruise altitude and a cruise to alternate for 200nm

Source:adapted from DLR-LY-IL Performance Tool, J. Fuchte, 2011
Source:Getting to Grips with Aircraft Performance, N.N., Airbus, 2002, pp. 174

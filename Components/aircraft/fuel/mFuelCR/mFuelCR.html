Parameter: mFuelCR¶

The fuel used in the cruise segment

Unit:[kg]

Calculation Methods¶

mFuelCR.calc()¶

If the design range Parameter: desRange is fixed calcFixRange is chosen, if not the calcFixFuel() method is selected.

mFuelCR.calcFixFuel()¶

Calculates the fuel mass for a cruise segment if the fuel mission fuel mass is fixed.

Since the mission fuel mass is fixed, the fuel mass for the cruise phase is limited by the fuel needed in all other segments of the mission.

Source:Daniel Boehnke

mFuelCR.calcFixRange()¶

Calculates the fuel mass for the cruise segment if the range is given.

The calculation is based on a simple equilibrium of forces approach. This means that this is a constant altitude cruise phase. An iteration cycle with a leg length of 10km is applied to find the needed fuel mass.

The initial aircraft mass is determined from the actual takeoff mass minus the fuel mass for climb and takoff

Source:adapted from DLR-LY-IL Performance Tool, J. Fuchte, 2011
Source:Getting to Grips with Aircraft Performance, N.N., Airbus, 2002, pp. 129

Parameter: mFM¶

The complete mission fuel mass

Unit:[kg]

Calculation Methods¶

mFM.calc()¶

Sets the calc method. If the Parameter: mTOM is fixed calcMass is chosen. If the Parameter: desRange is fixed calcSum is chosen.

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mFuelCLIMB
  • Parameter: mFuelDESCENT
  • Parameter: mFuelCR
  • Parameter: mFuelTO
  • Parameter: mFuelRES
Sensitivities:
mFM.calcMass()¶

Calculates the mission fuel mass from the maximum takeoff mass and the operating empty weight and payload

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mTOM
  • Parameter: oEM
  • Parameter: mPayload
Sensitivities:
mFM.calcRaymer()¶

Calculate the fuel needed for the complete mission via Brequet equation and fuel fractions

Source:Aircraft Design: A Conceptual Approach, D. P. Raymer, AIAA Education Series, 1992, Second Edition, p. 19
Dependencies:
  • Parameter: TASCR
  • Parameter: desRange
  • Parameter: sfcCR
  • Parameter: loDCR
  • Parameter: timeRES
  • Parameter: sfcLOI
  • Parameter: loDLOI
  • Parameter: oEM
  • Parameter: mPayload
Sensitivities:
mFM.calcSum()¶

Calculate the fuel needed for the complete mission via the sum of all mission segment fuel masses

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mFuelCLIMB
  • Parameter: mFuelDESCENT
  • Parameter: mFuelCR
  • Parameter: mFuelTO
  • Parameter: mFuelRES
Sensitivities:
mFM.calcViaRange()¶

Calculates the mission fuel mass for for an electric airplane where no mission fuel is lost during cruise

Author:Marco Friederich, TU Braunschweig

CPACS Import¶

The values for mFM are imported from:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <fuel>
                     <massDescription>
                        <mass>

CPACS Export¶

The values for mFM are exported to:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <fuel>
                     <massDescription>
                        <mass>

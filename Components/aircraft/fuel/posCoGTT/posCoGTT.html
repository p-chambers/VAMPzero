Parameter: posCoGTT¶

X Position of the center of gravity of the trim tank

Unit:[m]

Calculation Methods¶

posCoGTT.calc()¶

Calculates the posCoG of the trim tank. The cog of the fuel tank will be assumed to be constant for all filling levels.

Parameter: mFuelDESCENT¶

The fuel used in the descent segment

Unit:[kg]

Calculation Methods¶

mFuelDESCENT.calc()¶

Calculates the fuel mass for the descent segment.

The descent segment is initialized at cruise altitude and continues down to FL1500. Masses and atmosphere data at these locations are taken into account. Equilibrium of forces is then applied to find the necessary thrust and calculate the needed fuel mass.

Source:adapted from DLR-LY-IL Performance Tool, J. Fuchte, 2011
Source:Getting to Grips with Aircraft Performance, N.N., Airbus, 2002, pp. 159

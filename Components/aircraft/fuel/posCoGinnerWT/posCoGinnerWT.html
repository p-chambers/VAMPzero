Parameter: posCoGinnerWT¶

X Position of the center of gravity of the inner wing tank

Unit:[m]

Calculation Methods¶

posCoGinnerWT.calc()¶

Calculates the posCoG of the inner wing tank. The cog of the fuel tank will be assumed to be constant for all filling levels.

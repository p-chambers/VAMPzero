Parameter: sLFL¶

The landing field length

Unit:[m]

Calculation Methods¶

sLFL.calc()¶

Calculates the landing field length from the wing loading and the maximum lift at landing. All calculations are on sea level

Source:Airplane Design Part I, J. Roskam, DARCorporation, 2005, Fourth Edition, p.111, eq. 3.15-3.16

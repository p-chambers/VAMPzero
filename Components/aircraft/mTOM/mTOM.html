Parameter: mTOM¶

The maximum take-off mass for the aircraft

Unit:[kg]

Calculation Methods¶

mTOM.calc()¶

Calculates the maximum take-off mass from the the operating empty mass, the mission fuel mass and the payload mass

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: oEM
  • Parameter: mFM
  • Parameter: mPayload
Sensitivities:

CPACS Import¶

The values for mTOM are imported from:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <designMasses>
                     <mTOM>
                        <mass>

CPACS Export¶

The values for mTOM are exported to:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <designMasses>
                     <mTOM>
                        <mass>

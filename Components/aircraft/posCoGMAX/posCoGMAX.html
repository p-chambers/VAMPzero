Parameter: posCoGMAX¶

The maximum x position of the center of gravity of the aircraft. It is calculated considering different combinations of fuel and payload.

Unit:[m]
Author:Patrick Goden, Technische Universitaet Hamburg Harburg, Master Thesis

Calculation Methods¶

posCoGMAX.calc()¶

Calculates the maximum x location for the overall center of gravity for the aircraft.

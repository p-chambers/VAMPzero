Parameter: refArea¶

The reference area of the wing

Unit:[m2]
Wiki:http://adg.stanford.edu/aa241/wingdesign/winggeometry.html

Calculation Methods¶

refArea.calc()¶

Calculates the reference area of the wing for given aspect ratio and span depending on the status of span and aspect ratio

If the span and the aspect ratio are fixed calcGeom is chosen else calcSizing is chosen

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mTOM
  • Parameter: wsMAX
Sensitivities:
refArea.calcGeom()¶

Calculates the reference area of the wing for given aspectRatio and span

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: span
  • Parameter: aspectRatio
Sensitivities:
refArea.calcSizing()¶

Calculates the reference area of the wing for maximum wing loading

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mTOM
  • Parameter: wsMAX
Sensitivities:

CPACS Import¶

The values for refArea are imported from:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <reference>
               <area>

CPACS Export¶

The values for refArea are exported to:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <reference>
               <area>

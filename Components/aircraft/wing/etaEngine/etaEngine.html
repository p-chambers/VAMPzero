Parameter: etaEngine¶

The eta coordinate of the engine on the wing. This parameter is recognised for the export of wing geometries

Unit:[ ]

Calculation Methods¶

etaEngine.calc()¶

Calculates the etaEngine paramter from the y position of the engine and the span of the wing.

Parameter: cRoot¶

Calculates the root chord of the aileron (note: only one aileron is considered).

Unit:[m]
Source:
Author:Lisanne van Veen

Calculation Methods¶

cRoot.calc()¶

The reference area of the aileron and the span of the aileron could be used to calculate the value of the average chord length. Due to the linear taper ratio the average chord length is located in the middle. (cTip + cRoot)/2 = cAVG The other relation between the root chord and the tip chord: cTip = taperRatioAileron*cRoot. The abovementioned equations are then used to calculate cRoot en cTip.

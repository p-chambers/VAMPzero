Parameter: zRoot¶

The z-position of the horizontal tail plane at leading edge, root

Unit:[m]

Calculation Methods¶

zRoot.calc()¶

Calculates the Z position of the horizontal tail depending on the location value. If the location value of the engine is equal to 1, configuration is conventional. else it is T-tail.

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: dfus
Sensitivities:
zRoot.calcConventional()¶

Calculates the horizontal tailplane’s z position relative to the fuselage diameter

Source:Improvements on a Very Simple Preliminary Aircraft Design Model, F. Dorbath, Airbus FPO, 2008, p. 44
Dependencies:
  • Parameter: dfus
Sensitivities:

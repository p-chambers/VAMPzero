Parameter: rootYLocation¶

Calculates the spanwise root location of the elevator, measured from the fuselage center line (note: only one elevator is considered).

Unit:[m]
Source:
Author:Lisanne van Veen

Calculation Methods¶

rootYLocation.calc()¶

The function is a statistical relation obtained by analyzing data of large passenger aircraft. The design space of this equation is:

  • spanWing 34.10 - 79.80 m
  • spanHtp 12.45 - 30.37 m
  • refAreaHtp 31 - 222.57 m2

Parameter: taperRatio¶

The taper ratio of the horizontal tailplane

Unit:[ ]

Calculation Methods¶

taperRatio.calc()¶

Calculates the taper ratio of the horizontal tail depending on the location value. If the location value of the engine is equal to 1, configuration is conventional. else it is T-tail.

Parameter: mLM¶

The maximum landing mass for the aircraft

Unit:[kg]

Calculation Methods¶

mLM.calc()¶

Calculates the maximum landing mass from the maximum take-off mass, maximum zero fuel mass and the range

Source:Synthesis of Subsonic Airplance Design, E. Torenbeek, Delft University Press, 1982, p. 275, Eq. 8-2
Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mZFW
  • Parameter: mTOM
  • Parameter: desRange
Sensitivities:

CPACS Import¶

The values for mLM are imported from:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <designMasses>
                     <mMLM>
                        <mass>

CPACS Export¶

The values for mLM are exported to:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <designMasses>
                     <mMLM>
                        <mass>

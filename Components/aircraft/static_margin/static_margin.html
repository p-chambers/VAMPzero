Parameter: static_margin¶

The distance from the aerdynamic centre of the aircraft and the posCoGMAX. Calculated by the gradient of the moment to the lift coefficient.

Unit:[-]

Calculation Methods¶

static_margin.calc()¶

Calculates the static margin for the maximum reward position of gravity, so that the aircraft is neutral stable.

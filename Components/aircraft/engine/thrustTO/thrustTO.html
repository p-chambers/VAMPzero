Parameter: thrustTO¶

Takeoff thrust for one engine. Conditions for airport level

Unit:[N]

Calculation Methods¶

thrustTO.calc()¶

chooses the appropriate calculation method for the thrust at takeoff depending on the status of the takeoff field length

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mTOM
  • Parameter: twTO
  • Parameter: nEngine
Sensitivities:
thrustTO.calcThrustCR()¶

Calculates the takeoff thrust backwards from the cruise thrust

Source:TU Berlin Aircraft Design Course I
Dependencies:
  • Parameter: bypassRatio
  • Parameter: thrustCR
  • Parameter: rhoAP
  • Parameter: rhoCR
  • Parameter: MaCR
Sensitivities:
thrustTO.calcThrustISA()¶

Calculates the takeoff thrust from the takeoff thrust at ISA conditions

Source:Uebungen im Fach Flugzeugbau, K. Kuhlmann, RWTH Aachen, 2006/2007, p. 10, eq. 30
Dependencies:
  • Parameter: thrustTOISA
  • Parameter: TAP
  • Parameter: pAP
Sensitivities:
thrustTO.calctwTO()¶

Calculates the takeoff thrust from the thrust to weight ratio!

The thrust to weight ratios from FAR are calculated to standard atmosphere at SL and deltaT = 0.

This calculation does take the airport level and deltaT into account.

Source:Airplane Design Part II, J. Roskam, DARCorporation, 2005, Fourth Edition, p. 98
Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mTOM
  • Parameter: twTO
  • Parameter: nEngine
Sensitivities:

CPACS Import¶

The values for thrustTO are imported from:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <engines>
         <engine>
            <analysis>
               <thrust00>

CPACS Export¶

The values for thrustTO are exported to:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <engines>
         <engine>
            <analysis>
               <thrust00>

Parameter: twFAR25121b¶

The thrust to weight ratio for the second segment climb

The configuration is:

  • Take-off Flaps extended
  • Landing Gear is up
  • Full thrust on remaining engines
  • V = V2
  • mTOM
Unit:[kg/m2]
Wiki:http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thrust-to-weight_ratio

Calculation Methods¶

Calculates the Thrust to Weight ratio for the second segment climb

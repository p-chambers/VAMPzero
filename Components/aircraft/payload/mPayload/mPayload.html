Parameter: mPayload¶

The payload mass consisiting of cargo and passengers and passenger luggage

Unit:[kg]

Calculation Methods¶

mPayload.calc()¶

Calculates the payload mass by adding up passenger weight, baggage weight and cargo

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: mCargo
  • Parameter: mPax
Sensitivities:

CPACS Import¶

The values for mPayload are imported from:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <payload>
                     <massDescription>
                        <mass>

CPACS Export¶

The values for mPayload are exported to:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <payload>
                     <massDescription>
                        <mass>

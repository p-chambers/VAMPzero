Parameter: mCargo¶

The mass of additional cargo

Unit:[kg]

Calculation Methods¶

mCargo.calc()¶

Calculates the mass of additional cargo from mPayload and paxSeats

CPACS Import¶

The values for mCargo are imported from:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <payload>
                     <mCargo>
                        <massDescription>
                           <mass>

CPACS Export¶

The values for mCargo are exported to:

<cpacs>
   <vehicles>
      <aircraft>
         <model>
            <analyses>
               <massBreakdown>
                  <payload>
                     <mCargo>
                        <massDescription>
                           <mass>

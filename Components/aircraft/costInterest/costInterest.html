Parameter: costInterest¶

The interest costs per flight hour

Unit:[EU/h]

Calculation Methods¶

costInterest.calc()¶

The calculation method stems from the QUICE project. As the interest cost are included in the annuity that is also influenced by the depreciation period, this calculation simply sets the interest cost to zero. See the corresponding calculation method for costDepreciation for further information.

Source:Analyse und Vergleich von DOC-Modellen zur Etablierung eines gemeinsam genutzten Rechenmodells bei Airbus und Universitaeten, M. Weiss, 2008
costInterest.calcKundu()¶

Calculates the interest costs per flight hour

The calculation is simplified by assuming that the total capital cost are similar to the aircraft price

Source:Aircraft Design, A. Kundu, 2010, p.546

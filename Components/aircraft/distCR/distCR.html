Parameter: distCR¶

The distance traveled during the cruise segment

Unit:[m]

Calculation Methods¶

distCR.calc()¶

Sets ths calc method for the calculation of the distance traveled in the cruise segment depending on the status of the design range. If the design range is fixed calcFixRange is chosen, else calcFixFuel is chosen.

Parameter: xRoot¶

The position of the vertical tail in x in absolute coordinates at the root leading edge

Calculation Methods¶

xRoot.calc()¶

Calculates the position of the vertical tail LHT should be as large as possible for VTP Therefore the position of the VTP is to be positioned at the rear of the fuselage

Source:Aircraft Design: A Conceptual Approach, D. P. Raymer, AIAA Education Series, 1992, Second Edition, p.111
Dependencies:
  • Parameter: lfus
  • Parameter: cRoot
Sensitivities:

CPACS Import¶

xRoot.cpacsImport(path='.\\cpacs.xml', TIXIHandle=None, TIGLHandle=None)¶

Retrieves the xRoot.py coordinate from TIGL. It will go for the vtp. The first sections x coordinate is read at eta and chord equal 0.

Parameter: phi25¶

The angle of sweep which characterizes a swept wing is conventionally measured along the 25% chord line. If the 25% chord line varies in sweep angle, the leading edge is used; if that varies, the sweep is expressed in sections (e.g., 25 degrees from 0 to 50% span, 15 degrees from 50% to wingtip).

Unit:[deg]
Wiki:http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swept_wing

Calculation Methods¶

phi25.calc()¶

Sets the vertical tailplanes 25% chord sweep angle to the according wing angle plus 5 degrees

Dependencies:
  • Parameter: phi25
Sensitivities:
phi25.calcDorbath()¶

Sets the vertical tailplanes 25% chord sweep angle to 40deg

Source:Improvements on a Very Simple Preliminary Aircraft Design Model, F. Dorbath, Airbus FPO, 2008, p. 54

CPACS Import¶

phi25.cpacsImport(path='.\\cpacs.xml', TIXIHandle=None, TIGLHandle=None)¶

Imports the quarter chord sweep angle of the vtp from TIGL. It takes the vtp’s first and last section and reads y and y coordinates and from these calculates the vtp sweep.

Parameter: priceAircraft¶

The market price of the aircraft including engines. The market price of an aircraft is usually way lower than the list price. As this price is important for the airline’s cost it is included here

Unit:[EU]

Calculation Methods¶

priceAircraft.calc()¶

The calculation method stems from the QUICE project. It calculates the aircraft price from a fixed price per kg oEM

Source:Analyse und Vergleich von DOC-Modellen zur Etablierung eines gemeinsam genutzten Rechenmodells bei Airbus und Universitaeten, M. Weiss, 2008
Dependencies:
  • Parameter: oEM
Sensitivities:
priceAircraft.calcPloetner()¶

Calculates the engine price from a regression applied to the Airliner Price Guide. The idiosyncratic error is neglected

Source:Influence of aircraft parameters on aircraft market price, K. Ploetner, M. Cole, M. Hornung, A. Isikveren, P. Wesseler, C. Essling, DLRK, 2012, table 3
